About Us

Who Are We?

For The Table is a proud tabletop gaming retailer, specializing in tabletop, card, and board games — from Dungeons & Dragons and Magic: The Gathering to Gloomhaven and more. We’re based in Melrose Arch (Johannesburg) and Menlyn Park (Pretoria), and always growing.

Our History

Born from two passionate gamers who fell in love with the vast worlds that tabletop and TCGs have to offer, we set out to make the hobby more accessible than ever, and now seek to bring those same experiences to as many people as possible. From a small Sandton storefront to two locations and an online home — the journey continues.

Our Goal

We’re aiming to be more than just a shop — we’re looking to forge a powerful community. Look forward to in-person events, beginner sessions, and online gatherings. Make new friends, discover new games, and craft epic memories.
