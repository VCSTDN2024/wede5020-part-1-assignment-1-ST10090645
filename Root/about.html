About Us

Who Are We?

For The Table is a proud tabletop gaming retailer, specializing in tabletop, card, and board games — from Dungeons & Dragons and Magic: The Gathering to Gloomhaven and more. We’re based in Melrose Arch (Johannesburg) and Menlyn Park (Pretoria), and always growing.

Our History

Born from two passionate gamers after COVID-19, we set out to make the hobby more accessible than ever. From a small Sandton storefront to two locations and an online home — the journey continues.

Our Goal

We’re more than a shop — we’re a community. Expect in-person events, beginner sessions, and online gatherings. Make new friends, discover new games, and forge epic memories.
