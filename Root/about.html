    Who are we?

    For The Table is a proud tabletop gaming retailer, who specializes in stocking and redistributing a vast collection of tabletop, card and board games, such as Dungeons & Dragons, Magic: The Gathering, Gloomhaven, and much more.
    We have two locations, Melrose Arch, Johannesburg, as well as Menlyn Park Shopping Centre, Pretoria and we strive to expand our ever growing collection of the latest games in order to continue being your go-to supplier.

    Our History

    Our business started off as two passionate tabletop gamers, who, after the wake of the global COVID-19 pandemic, sought to revitalize the tabletop gaming hobby by making it more accessible and easy to get into than ever. Thus, For the Table was born, a small, niche shop in a quiet corner of Sandton, has now grown at a modest pace and now has two locations across the country, as well as a new website/online store (that's where you are right now!).

    Our Goal

    Our hope and goal is to not only be a reliable supplier but also foster a life-changing hobby in many others that brings people together and helps create epic memories. Look forward to exciting in-person events, as well as some digital online events for those who are a little shy, where you'll hopefully find new friends and new games to add to your game nights!
